For students

Time schedule

Meet companies from IT sphere and win interesting prices! Which projects are companies solving? Where can you gain experience? Do not miss opportunity to get to know companies right in university campus. We are looking forward to seeing you!

  1. 12:30 - 18:00
    Atrium of The New Building

    Informational kiosks of companies

    Visit informational kiosks of FIT partners and sponsors. Ask for what are you interested in. What projects are companies solving currently? Which students are they looking for cooperation? What can they offer to you? Get valuable information and connections during all the afternoon!

    Informational kiosk of SSP portal

    This kiosk serves as an informational kiosk of Co@FIT. SSP portal provides cooperation between students and companies where can companies offer assignments as semestral projects or thesis.

  2. 13:30 - 14:00
    Lecture Hall T9:107

    How to cooperate with industry

    Learn how to cooperate with industry through SSP portal.

  3. 16:45 - 18:00
    Lecture Hall T9:111

    Presentations of FIT partners

    Do not miss the presentations of our golden and silver partners. They will talk about job opportunities and interesting projects which the companies are working on, which assignments they offer in SSP portal or what skills from student they require.

    Drawing FIT-raffle

    During presentations will be drawing a raffle. Win electronics sponsored by our partners!
