About Co@FIT

Faculty of Information Technology holds event Co@FIT (Companies at FIT) - meeting of students with companies. The purpose is students to mediate contact with an industrial sector, especially to long-term industrial partners of FIT and enable job opportunities.

Career fair Co@FIT brings SSP portal in cooperation with companies for the fifth time. This winter semester 2016/2017 it will take place in the atrium of The New Building of Faculty of Information Technology. Informational kiosk of SSP portal will be ready for students and companies. There you can get more information about Co@FIT and sign in to SSP.

During all day there will be kiosks of industrial partners in the atrium of The New Building where students can get information about working in companies, technologies and programming languages, requirements for knowledge and promotional materials. This event is an special occasion for students to get connect with companies, learn interesting facts and settle their semester projects, thesis or dissertation. It is suitable and recommended to students from all fields of FIT. Afternoon in conclusion there are arranged short lectures of our golden and silver partners and also FIT-raffle.

Questions and Answers to Co@FIT

Where and when to come?

Kiosks will be ready at 11:00 in the atrium of The New Building so you will have time to arrange your promotional materials. When you arrive in the building there will be our support team who can help you during the event. Program officially starts at 12:30.

Where can I park?

Parking is available only in paid parking zones. There are no available parking places at the FIT building.

What will be available at the faculty?

You will receive information about Wifi connection before event on your e-mail address, eventually on the kiosk SSP. There are provided extension cords for your electronic devices. Professional photographer will document the entire event. Photo report will be available for you. There will be prepared also refreshments (room 153). Water is provided in kiosks.

What does "informational kiosk" mean?

Informational kiosks are office desks with chairs. Each desk is signed with a company name, logo and short description (your technologies and programming languages). You can bring roll-ups and promotional materials. We recommend preparing assignments or themes of semester projects and thesis for SSP portal.

How should I prepare my presentation as a FITpartner?

As a golden/silver partner you have a chance to introduce your company during the main program for students. Golden partners have 15 minutes for presentation, silver partners have 10 minutes. Content of the presentation is on you, but you can inspire yourself by points below. We ask you to attend the whole lecture. Projector, screen and sound system will be available in the lecture room.

Recommended points of your presentation:
  • About your company
  • Successful and interesting projects (connected with SSP portal and also beyond)
  • Which projects you can offer to students
  • What are your requirements on students (bachelor, master, skills they should know)
  • What can student expect from cooperation with your company (development opportunities, benefits, corporate culture etc.)

How many students are expected to visit Co@FIT?

About 500 students are expected to come.

Where do I get more information?

Assistant of Co@FIT is Lucie Kolomazníková (lucie.kolomaznikova@fit.cvut.cz) and she can provide you information. During Co@FIT there is our support team and informational kiosk SSP.

Partnership and sponsoring

Faculty of Information Technology targets to educate highly specialized IT professionals as a main element of innovative potential of companies and institutions especially in Czech Republic. We are systematically building scientific research capacities which enable us to teach students the latest trends and knowledge but also to offer expertise consulting and realize projects in cooperation with companies and institutions.

Solid and long-term relationships with industrial partners belong to the historical tradition of the Czech Technical University in Prague. At the Faculty of Information Technology we are trying to make cooperation with industrial sphere as mutually beneficial as possible. Industrial partners are not only the target sphere for our absolvents but also important partners for forming our studying programs and scientifical researches and activities. To improve and systematize this cooperation we have prepared program of partnership called FITpartner. FITpartner enables companies and institutions to influence future development and specialization of our students but also it is possible close cooperation on researches. This program has various forms of partnership divided according commitment of companies to the faculty.

For more information please contact Department of Development.

SSP portal

The purpose of SSP portal is to enable effective and useful cooperation of companies and students.

Many students are employed during their studies. But their work is not usually connected to school although it might have been. Thanks to SSP portal it is possible to solve this problem and connect part-time jobs with school duties. So students can cooperate with companies and take part on interesting projects from IT, develop skills and learn programming languages and technologies used in practise.

We would like to support interesting projects and create nice place to cooperate together on new knowledge come in concrete innovations. This system gives to the Faculty strong feedback which is used to improve practical parts of subjects.

Pros for industrial partner (company) are opportunities to cooperate with young talents and forming their future development, using expertise consulting and experiences teachers during solving assignments and testing, give semestral work or thesis to students. Student can benefit from contact and cooperation with company, use opportunities for practise in IT and get experience on interesting projects in connection with school attendance and duties.

What new is planned in CWI?

We are constantly thinking of new ways of improving the portal and still working on development of new functions. We’d like to inform you about some of them.

First, we want to increase a support of faculty experts who help to industrial partners with creating assignments, provide expert advise and can lead work teams. Next change will be that other users (students, teachers and experts) can suggest their own assignments and requirements. After that industrial partners (companies) could pick the assignment which they are interested in and want to sponsor. We can’t forget to mention making of redesign of front page of portal SSP. It will make your work with portal easier, quicker and keeps you informed about all news. Stay tuned!
