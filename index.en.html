About Co@FIT

Faculty of Information Technology holds event Co@FIT (Companies at FIT) - meeting of companies agents and students. This year it is the third annual event already. Purpose of Co@FIT is students to mediate contact with industrial sector, especially to our long-term industrial partners. In this semester our partners(Profinit, Lely, SuseLinux, Mikenopa, Hewlett-Packard, Komerční banka, Barclays, Gemalto, Ackee) and many other companies will take part.

This semester Co@FIT takes place on 15. 10. 2015 at 12:30 in Faculty of Information Technology. Informational kiosk of Cooperation with Industry portal (CWI), will be ready for students and industrial partners in the atrium of the building. This kiosk serves as an opening and informational kiosk of Co@FIT.

During Co@FIT there are kiosks of industrial partners in the atrium where students can get information about working in companies, used technologies, programming language, requirements for knowledge and also promotional materials. Students can connect to companies, learn interesting facts and settle their semester projects, thesis or dissertation.

From 16:30 there are arranged lectures of our golden (Profinit, Lely Industries) and silver (SuseLinux, Mikenopa, Hewlett-Packard, Komerční banka) partners in the lecture hall 107. During lectures of the partners students can win some of valuable prizes.

Questions and Answers to Co@FIT

Where and when to come?

At 11:30 kiosks will be ready for you so you will have plenty of time to arrange your promotional materials. When you arrives in the building there will be our team and they help you and advise you. Our team wears black FIT t-shirts with CTU logo. Program officialy starts at 12:30 for students.

Where can I park?

You can park in the streets Bechyňova, Bílá, Kadeřávkovská, Flemingovo náměstí. There are no available parking places at the FIT building.

What will be available at the faculty?

You will receive information about Wifi connection before event on your e-mail address, eventually on kiosk CWI. There are provided extension cords for your electronic devices. The entire event photographer will document the event and a photoreport will be available for you.

What about refreshments?

There are prepared refreshments for you in the room 153. Water is provided on the kiosks.

What does "informational kiosk" mean?

Informational kiosks are office desks in the atrium. Each company has its own desk with chairs signed with a company name, a logo and short describtion (technologies, programming languages). You can bring roll-ups and promotional materials. Also we recommend preparing assignments or themes of semester projects and thesis for CWI portal.

How should I prepare my presentation as a FITpartner?

As a golden/silver partner you have a chance to introduce your company during the main program for students. Golden partners have 15 minutes for presentation, silver partners have 10 minutes. Content of the presentation is on you but it would be useful if you follow recommended points. We ask you to attend whole lecture (main program). Projector, screen and sound system will be available in the lecture room.

Recommended points of your presentation:
  • What your company does
  • successful and interesting projects (connected with CWI portal and also beyond)
  • which projects you can offer to students
  • what students do you find and need and your requirements on them (bachelor students, master students, which skills they should know)
  • what can student expect from cooperation with your company (benefits, development opportunities, corporate culture and philosophy etc.)

How many students are expected to visit Co@FIT?

About 500 students are expected to come.

Where do I get more information?

If you need more information or have a problem, contact our team from informational kiosk Cooperation with Industry portal.

About program FITpartner

Solid and long-term relationships with industrial partners belong to the historical tradition of the Czech Technical University in Prague. At the Faculty of Information Technology we are trying to make cooperation with industrial sphere as mutually beneficial as possible. Industrial partners are not only target sphere for our absolvents but also important partner for forming our studying programs and scientifical researches and activities. To improve and systematize this cooperation we offer program of partnership called FITpartner. This program has various forms of partnership divided according commitment of companies to the faculty.

For more information contact please Department of Development.
